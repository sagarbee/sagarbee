### 🚀 About Me

I'm a backend developer with 2+ years of experience building secure, high-throughput
payment gateway systems — spanning iframe & hosted-paypage integrations, wallet processing,
and PCI-DSS compliant transaction flows across merchant-facing payment channels.

My work sits mostly at the **security and reliability layer** of payment infrastructure:

- 🛡️ Designing **real-time, rule-based fraud detection engines** that flag and block
  suspicious transactions before they're processed
- 🔐 Securing payment payloads with **HMAC authentication, AES-256, and RSA encryption**
- ⚙️ Scaling configuration systems — from file-based **YAML with hot-reload**, to
  **distributed caching (Infinispan) with MariaDB persistence** — as transaction volume grows
- 🧩 Leading **JDK upgrades** and deploying backend services on **Payara Server (Jakarta EE)**
  with a focus on TPS throughput and production stability

I care about building systems that are secure *and* boring in the best way — predictable,
observable, and hard to break.
